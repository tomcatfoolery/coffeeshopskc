Eleos Coffee House

Forget pretentious prices when patronizing this gem in Northeast Kansas City. I got a loaded grilled cheese sandwich with a side of potato salad for $7.50 plus tax. Sure beats paying $8 for a cucumber/mint infused soda. The west-facing window has a delightfully spacious work area with a power strip on top for convenient access. There is plenty of seating with 3 different types–a middle area with tables and chairs, a booth-type set up against the east wall, and the west-facing work area I mentioned above. I received a $10 gift card, so I checked this place out, and I’m happy I did.
